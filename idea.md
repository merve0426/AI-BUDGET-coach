# AI Budget Coach

## Problem
Many students and young adults struggle to manage their income and expenses effectively. Daily spending accumulates over time, and users often cannot clearly understand where their money goes. This leads to low financial awareness, unnecessary spending, and difficulty in saving money.

## User
- University students  
- New graduates  
- Young individuals who want to better manage their finances  
- Users with basic financial knowledge  

## Role of AI
The AI plays a central role in this application. It analyzes the user’s income and expense data, categorizes spending, identifies unnecessary or excessive expenses, and provides personalized savings recommendations. It also generates a financial health score and a simple budget improvement plan, acting as a personal financial coach.

## Competitor Analysis
There are existing budgeting applications such as Mint and YNAB. However, these tools mainly focus on tracking expenses and require manual interpretation by the user.  
AI Budget Coach differentiates itself by:
- Providing AI-driven financial analysis  
- Offering personalized recommendations  
- Automatically identifying unnecessary spending  
- Delivering a simple and user-friendly financial coaching experience  

## Success Criteria
The project is considered successful if:
- Users can easily input their financial data  
- The AI provides meaningful and personalized analysis  
- Spending is clearly categorized  
- Users can identify unnecessary expenses  
- Users receive actionable saving recommendations  
- Results are generated within 10–15 seconds
